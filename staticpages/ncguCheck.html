Non-Commercial Group Use

A noncommercial group use is any activity on National Forest System lands:

  1. That involves 75 or more people, either as participants or spectators;
  2. Where an entry or participation fee is not charged; and
  3. Where the primary purpose is not the sale of a good or service (36 CFR 251.51).
